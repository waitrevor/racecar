Free SGGEE Journal

To introduce you to the type of material available in our Journal, we are please to offer the March 2006 Journal (3760KB Adobe Acrobat format) at no cost. Also available is an index of all past issues current through September, 2013.

You must have Acrobat Reader to view the Journal. If necessary, see the Technical page on this site to obtain a current copy for your computer.

To view the Journal at any time at your convenience, save the file to your hard drive after opening it in Adobe Reader. When the Journal is saved to your hard drive, find the saved file and double click it to open the Journal in Adobe Acrobat.
