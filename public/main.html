Master Pedigree Database (MPD)

Updated June 15, 2024

The Master Pedigree Database has just been updated with the most current data received and processed to date. It has been merged resulting in 560,730 records all of which have been renumbered. This update now contains the kantorate files for Alt Kurgan, Amelin, Antonowka, Berestowiec, Horodischtsche, Kolowert I and II, Kurhany, Ludmilpol, Maksimilianowka, Rudenka, Szelesnica, and Totowicz.
