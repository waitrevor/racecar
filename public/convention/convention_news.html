    Hotel information for the 20th SGGEE conference next July 27-29 is now available. You may make your reservations at the conference rate below as long as rooms remain available or before June 26, 2018.

    Our venue is the Coast Plaza Hotel (1316 – 33rd Street NE) Calgary, Alberta, Canada T2A 6B6.

    Rates (taxes are not included):

    • Comfort Room (two queen beds) $109.00
    • Superior Room (one king bed) $134.00
    • Jacuzzi Suite $184.00
    • Executive Suite $204.00

    When booking your guestroom, please follow one of the following options:

    • Call the hotel directly at 1.403.248.8888 or toll free at 1.800.661.1464
    • Email your reservation request to res@calgaryplaza.com

    Please make sure to have your credit card ready and quote one of the following:

    • Group Name: Society For German Genealogy In Eastern Europe (SGGEE)
    • Reservation Number: GFC24101

    Program planning for the conference is under way. Our theme is “hands-on genealogy” to celebrate our society’s progress and successes in its first 20 years. And as always, to give participants an educational and fun weekend.

    Program suggestions or conference questions may be e-mailed to convention-sggee@googlegroups.com.
