Inglis Holy Trinity Lutheran Cemetery Records

The following data was extracted from a defunct website named "For Posterity's Sake".

Recorded and Photographed April 1st & 2nd, 2004

Located at the corner of Highway 366 and Railway Ave in Inglis, Manitoba.  This is a small, well kept rural cemetery.  There was still snow in areas of the cemetery and a second visit will be necessary to ensure all headstones are recorded.

Many of those buried in this cemetery were Volhynian Germans.
