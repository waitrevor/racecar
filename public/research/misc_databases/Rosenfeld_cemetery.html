Rosenfeld, MB Cemetery Records

The following data was compiled by Carol Castleman from personal visit to the site and other sources and is current to 2003.

The Rosenfeld village had, at one time, two Mennonite churches and one Lutheran church. The village cemetery was shared by all. Those with Lutheran background were primarily Germans from Volhynia.

The oldest internment appears to be in 1872/96 though the cemetery did not see active use until after 1900.
