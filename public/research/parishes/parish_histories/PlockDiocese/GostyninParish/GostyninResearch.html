Village of Huta near Gostynin

Courtesy of Ron Newman:
Earliest German entries for the village of Huta appeared to be in 1777. The following names were extracted from Gostynin Catholic Record microfilm (1496792) and appear to represent the earliest Lutheran German families there.

Christian/Eliz. Trojan; Karol/Florentina Kunkel; Michael/Anna Kunkel; Christian/Anna Lippert; Fryderic Zyc; Erdman/Margaretha Trojan; Christian Kunkel; Joannis/Bogumila Frank; Christian/Anna Hoff; Andreas Beyer; Christian/Anna Letin; Andreas/Anna Zyc; Michael/Marianna Hoff; Samuel/Florentina Hoff; Christian/Rosine Wenzel; Johann/Christine Kunkel; Christian/Magdalena Zyc; Jacob/Christine Kunkel;
