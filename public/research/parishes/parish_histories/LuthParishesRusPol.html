Lutheran Parishes in Russian Poland

Description

The following is a listing of all Lutheran Parishes in Russian Poland - first alphabetically by Diocese and then, within each Diocese, alphabetically by parish or affiliate.

We are attempting to compile three sections of information about each parish.

  • History: These histories are being prepared by summarizing existing German language material and translating into English. Other sources will be added as available.
  • Photos: Pictures can be of the church, people, or region and can be historical or modern.
  • More: Miscellaneous material to assist with research. Sample features might include local research tips, lists of people or surnames for specific villages, lists of Catholic Churches where records may contain info about Protestants living in the area, village maps, etc. Look here also for scans of documents which have origins in the particular parish.

It will take a lot of work to provide material for every parish but we will continue to work on it so come back often to look for new material.

Where you see (A) after the name, it indicates an affilate rather than a full parish. An affiliate has a church but is served by a pastor from another full parish.
