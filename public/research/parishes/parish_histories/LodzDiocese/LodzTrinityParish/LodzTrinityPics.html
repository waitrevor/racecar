German Lutheran Cemetery in Lodz
This cemetery contains numerous huge monuments to the German industrialists who, among others, were prominent in developing the cloth making industry in Lodz. Yet scattered among them one finds many other headstones representing family names that are being researched by members of SGGEE. While many German cemeteries in Poland have been abandoned to overgrowth and ruin, this one is somewhat well maintained with, of course some exceptions.

 

Many names on the well-kept markers are recognizable. Included here are Lange, Ende, Pokrant, Seidel, Hartwig, and Schultz.
