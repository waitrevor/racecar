Village of Gląbie/Glombie NNE of Nowosolna

Courtesy of Uta Härtling:
Gląbie is located about 7 km NNE of Nowosolna. The village was founded about 1798 and records through to 1819 are held in the Catholic Church at Skoszewy. In 1830, there were only about 10 homesteads in this village. The following is a list of German couples in this village as extracted from these Catholic records (LDS film 2084554).
