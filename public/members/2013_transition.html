2013 Proposed Transition to new Articles and By-Laws

SGGEE has received notice from Industry Canada requiring us to Transition our present Constitution and By-Laws registered under the Canada Corporations Act, Part II, to new Articles of continuance (transition) and By-Laws under the new Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act.

All corporations  incorporated under the old legislation must transition to the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act by October 17, 2014.

For your information the New Legislation Canada Not-for-Profit Corporations Act - Corporations Canada can be found at:
