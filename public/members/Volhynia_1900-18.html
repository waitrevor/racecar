Volhynian Archives Indexes 1900-1918

General Introduction

These newly transcribed indexes are a first point of access to the extensive collection of Lutheran parish birth, marriage and death records in the Zhytomyr Archive and Rowno Archive. The Zhytomyr Archive covers the parishes of Emilchin, Heimtal, Novograd-Volhynski, Radomyschl, and Zhytomyr for selected years in the period 1900 to 1919.  The Rowno Archive covers the parishes of Tutchin, Rowno and some from Kupla church. They are incomplete but nevertheless these records contain a considerable amount of useful information.

The following databases allow an advanced search for names, or parts of names, and Soundex.

Parish Registers

The original documents are in Russian, are kept in the Zhytomyr Archive and Rowno Archive, and are not online.

Parish registers in Zhytomyr Archives are found in -- Fond 67, opis 8, files 1-50

  • Heimtal: 1900, 1902-1903, 1906-1914
  • Emilchin: 1900, 1902–1903, 1906-1914
  • Novograd-Volynsk: 1900, 1902-1910, 1912-16, 1918-1919
  • Radomyschl: 1907-1915
  • Tutchin church book for year 1905 only, located in Zhytomyr Archives. Includes some births from 1890’s
  • Zhytomyr: 1900, 1902-1910, 1911-1915, 1917

Parish registers in Rowno Archives are found in -- Fond 740, opis 5, files 17-28

  • Tutchin: 1900, 1902-1904, 1906-1910, 1912-1915
  • Rowno: 1902-1907

Fond 639, opis 3, case 84

  • Kupla: 1888-1903 (sparse records)

PLEASE NOTE:
 The years shown are the years when a new church book was created. Many birth entries from different years are recorded as late as about 5 years after the birth. For example, a pre-1900 birth may be recorded in 1903 or 1905 or even later, depending on where the baptism took place.
