Original Records Indices

Parish Records Index (PRI) database (last update February 2012 - about 368,800 names in database)

SGGEE has created a searchable database of names and other vital statistics data from the indices of the original Polish and Russian civil and parish records. See Job Central for a listing of the parishes included. In some parishes the volunteers are only extracting names from the indices, while in other cases the volunteers are extracting all the data in the original record. Note also that our database includes some personal files from a few of our members, and that data contains some records from parishes that have not been entered into the Job Central listing of parishes being worked on.

Permission has now been received from the Polish Archives to show only the indices for these records on the SGGEE website. An effort is being made to convert those records that have more than just index information into the Pedigree database, but until that is done, you can continue to ask us to do a search of the master database to see if there is additional information on any names that are of interest to you. Just send an email to databases-sggee@googlegroups.com and be sure to mention that you want a specific search of the PRI database for a specific name, including the registration number and parish name.
