Volhynian Archives Indexes - 1900-1918

General Introduction

These newly transcribed indexes are a first point of access to the extensive collection of Lutheran parish birth, marriage and death records in the Zhytomyr Archive, Rowno Archive, and Lutzk (Volyn) Archive. The Zhytomyr Archive covers the parishes of Emilchin, Heimtal, Novograd-Volhynski, Radomyschl, and Zhytomyr for selected years in the period 1900 to 1919. The Rowno Archive covers the parishes of Tutchin, Rowno and some from Kupla church. The Lutzk (Volyn) Archive covers the parishes of Lutzk, Rozyszcze, Wladymir-Wolhynsk, with some records from Dunajewtzy and Nemirow. They are incomplete but nevertheless these records contain a considerable amount of useful information.

  • Births (father’s name, mother’s maiden name, date of birth, date of baptism, village where born, village where baptized, godparents),

  • Marriages (groom and bride’s family name, date of marriage, village name)

  • Deaths (age, village name, place of birth, father’s name, mother’s maiden name, spouse, cause of death).

The original documents are in Russian, are kept in the Zhytomyr Archive, Rowno Archive, and Lutzk (Volyn) Archive, and are not online.

PLEASE NOTE: The years shown are the years when a new church book was created. Many birth entries from different years are recorded as late as about 5 years after the birth. For example, a pre-1900 birth may be recorded in 1903 or 1905 or even later, depending on where the baptism took place.

Parish registers in Zhytomyr Archives are found in -- Fond 67, opis 8, files 1-50

    • Heimtal: 1900, 1902-1903, 1906-1914
    • Emilchin: 1900, 1902–1903, 1906-1914
    • Novograd-Volynsk: 1900, 1902-1910, 1912-16, 1918-1919
    • Radomyschl: 1907-1915
    • Tutchin church book for year 1905 only, located in Zhytomyr Archives. Includes some births from 1890’s
    • Zhytomyr: 1900, 1902-1910, 1911-1915, 1917

Parish registers in Rowno Archives are found in -- Fond 740, opis 5, files 17-28

    • Tutchin: 1900, 1902-1904, 1906-1910, 1912-1915
    • Rowno: 1902-1907

      Fond 639, opis 3, case 84
    • Kupla: 1888-1903 (sparse records)

Parish registers in Lutzk Archives are found in – Fond 35, opis 9, files 643, and 771-783.

    • Lutzk: 1899-1900, 1902-1903, 1906-1914
    • Rozyszcze: 1900, 1902-1903, 1906-1913
    • Wladymir-Wolhynsk: 1900, 1902-1903, 1906-1913
    • Dunajewtzy and Nemirow: 1914
