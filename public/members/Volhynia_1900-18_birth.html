Volhynia Archives Birth Indexes - 1900-1918

Volhynian Lutheran Parishes

These newly transcribed indexes are a first point of access to the extensive collection of Lutheran parish birth, marriage, and death records in the Zhytomyr Archive and Rowno Archive. The Zhytomyr Archive covers the parishes of Emilchin, Heimtal, Novograd-Volhynski, Radomyschl, and Zhytomyr for selected years in the period 1900 to 1919. The Rowno Archive covers the parishes of Tutchin, Rowno, and some from Kupla church. They are incomplete but nevertheless, these records contain a considerable amount of useful information.

This page allows you to do partial name searches such as searching for "chu" in the middle of a name as well as Soundex searching.

You can also search the Marriage and Death databases. Back to the Introductory Volhynian Archive page.
