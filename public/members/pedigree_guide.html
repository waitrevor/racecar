Privacy

In the interest of privacy, persons under the age of 80 who are living, are not named but are listed as LIVING. However, if data is submitted that does not include a birth date, the system cannot calculate how old those persons are. The system considers them to be “dead” and the name will show up in the search results. For submitters who do not want any information about living people to appear, please include at least a birth year in your submission to us. For more information, please refer to our detailed SGGEE privacy statement.

 

Proceed to the databases

        • Master Pedigree Database (includes SGGEE001r10 and Lodz Trinity Database noted below)
          Latest update October 5, 2014 (525,711 names) -type 1 and 2 data as noted above. Please note that not all data is merged yet so there might be duplication of names. The merging goes on behind the scenes throughout the year.

        • SGGEE001r10 database. This is a database of original Polish Lutheran records. Latest update July, 2009 (over 193,000 names)- type 2 data as noted above.

        • Lodz Trinity Pedigree database.
          Latest update August 5, 2007 (34,492 names)- type 2 data as noted above. This database is now completed. It includes the names from the original Polish Lutheran records of the Lodz Trinity church. Our thanks to John Marsch for his special labour of love in completing this project.

Inquiries can be made to databases-sggee@googlegroups.com to obtain the film number, and registration number for all names in the database that show a source of SGGEE001, SGGEE002, etc.
