Trinity Lutheran - Lodz, Poland

Search Trinity Lutheran - Lodz Database

This pedigree database is a partial compilation of the records from the Lodz Trinity Lutheran Church for the years 1826 to 1851, as originally extracted / translated by hand by Michael Radke. The database compilation into Legacy format by John Marsch is now completed.


John Marsch says the following: "The usual disclaimers apply to this file, of course. Check the SOURCE FILM is the watchword. Radke made mistakes, I have made mistakes, and the original recorder(s) made them as well. If considered necessary I (John) can check the Radke translations for members upon request. Surname spelling variations are the biggest bug-a-boo. In some cases, there are three or four surname differences for an individual, some are radical."
