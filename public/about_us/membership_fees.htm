    Register Using PayPal or Credit Card

    With PayPal or credit card, there is only one rate - $40 CDN

    To use a credit card, you will have to proceed through the PayPal process. Or you can use your own PayPal account.

    PayPal will automatically make currency conversion for you with no high bank fees.

    You do not need to register with PayPal directly if you are using a credit card. To make a direct deposit from your bank account, you will need a PayPal account.

    Important:

    You CANNOT make a PayPal payment via email. You MUST follow the "Online Registration Form" link for payment transfer to work correctly. You will be able to choose between paying with your credit card or through PayPal.

    Please make your PayPal payment through the SGGEE link to ensure secure payment. SGGEE does not accept responsibility for incorrect payment methods.
