Types of Databases

Type 1 Data - Member Submissions

This is, to the extent provided by our members, a listing of data on the descendants of those ancestors (generally German - Volhynia or German - Russian Poland). If you wish to contribute to this database, you can send your GEDCOM file to databases@sggee.org as an attachment. If you do so, we invite you to include all persons in your database that are in anyway connected to Germans from Poland or Volhynia, or their ancestors from any of the German provinces. We would ask however, that you either eliminate all lines that do not have such a connection, or at least advise us what those lines are so that we can eliminate them before we enter your data into our database. If you wish to participate with your data, please include yourself linked into the data (at least your name, including maiden name if married female) so that we can determine how you are related to others in the database, assuming your data shows a link to others.

We have a volunteer who compiles submitted data into our huge database. It is extremely helpful if you download our submittal instructions and follow them closely before you send it to us. This will greatly simplify the work this volunteer has to do. We would also encourage you to use common spellings and equivalencies where possible in accord with our given names equivalency list and our standard surnames list.

Remember also that, if you do not want any information about living people to appear, include at least a year of birth so that our system recognizes them. Refer also to the SGGEE privacy policy (pdf).

 

Type 2 Data - Original Records

These records have been entered into a genealogy program, and are thus linked to the extent that we have been able to discover links. We have tried our best to make only the links that are nearly certain, but, especialy as the database grows and additional merges are made, there may be errors in the database. Accordingly, you are advised that it is your responsibility to confirm that the data is both correct in content, and correct in its links to other records. Do not accept our work as definitive. If you have time to donate to this important work of extracting data from original records, please also send an email to databases@sggee.org.
